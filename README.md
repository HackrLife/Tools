hackrtools.com

## What is HackrTools?

HackrTools is a curated collection of tools built for people who move fast and think clearly. Whether you are modelling unit economics, stress-testing a mental model, planning a campaign, or automating a workflow — there is a tool here for that.

Everything runs in your browser. No data leaves your device.

---

## Why These Tools Exist

These are not generic tools pulled from a textbook.

After 15 years across growth, GTM, product, and demand generation roles at Google, SAP, Adobe, LinkedIn, and IBM, I built these tools for myself — to solve real problems on the job. They reflect how senior operators actually think about metrics, decisions, and execution, not how consultants present them in decks.

Every tool in this collection has been used in practice. The frameworks are the ones that held up under pressure. The calculators are built around the questions that actually get asked in the room.

HackrTools is what I wished existed when I was figuring this out.

## Tool Categories

### Growth Tools
Calculators and models for founders and operators — unit economics, SaaS metrics, pricing sensitivity, funnel analysis, and more.

### Mental Model Frameworks
Interactive frameworks that turn abstract thinking tools into structured decision aids. First principles, inversion, second-order thinking, and beyond.

### Digital Marketing Tools
Practical tools for campaign planning, content strategy, SEO analysis, audience segmentation, and performance estimation.

### SOPs
Structured standard operating procedures for repeatable growth and marketing tasks — formatted for real teams, not theory.

### Agentic Tools
Lightweight AI-powered tools for research, synthesis, and automation. Built for operators who want leverage without complexity.
